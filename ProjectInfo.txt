Project Name=Football Manager


An application/game for football enthusiasts for managing their favourite football team. The app provides support for being a manager of a particular club and purchasing favourite players into your football club.

Features:

Can buy players directly from the "players list" using a manager.

Players need to be given proper salaries otherwise they wont join your club.

Some popular managers available:
1.Jose Mourinho
2.Pep Gourdiola

Some popular players:
1.Cristiano Ronaldo
2.David Beckham
